About Me!

Hi there! My name is Benjamin! I'm a 3 year old Rhodesian Ridegeback and I am known as the troublemaker of the house. Whenever I get bored I like to steal socks and other clothing from my humans and hide it for them to find later. When I'm not stealing clothing, I'm stealing any food that gets in reach. I'm a big boy, I already weigh over 100 pounds and can reach most counter tops by just reaching my neck up. During the day I mostly like to sleep and watch TV with my humans or steal items around the house and make the humans chase me around to get them back. There's also no door that can hold me back either! Some might tell me I'm too smart for my own good since I know how to open doors and will come and go as I please when I want attention. I love to run around the house and play tag but I hate to exercize. Sometimes when my humans take me out for a walk I decide I'm too tired and make one of them come drive me home. It's a rough life being a dog! They understand right?
